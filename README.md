# Problem Definition

## Background

Creative industries rely heavily on human expertise, strategic thinking, and collaboration. However, creative teams frequently encounter bottlenecks that reduce efficiency and creative quality.

## Key Challenges

1. Unclear client briefs
2. Misalignment between business objectives and creative concepts
3. Difficulty challenging ideas objectively
4. Repeated revision cycles
5. Limited ability to predict audience and stakeholder reactions

## Opportunity

Agentic AI can provide structured creative intelligence by supporting analysis, critique, and decision-making throughout the creative process.
